# Competitor & Similar Sites Research

> **Date:** April 2026
> **Scope:** Norwegian digital agencies and tech studios offering AI solutions, custom software, and creative digital services — with focus on companies comparable to Falck Studios.

---

## Executive Summary

Falck Studios operates in a growing Norwegian market for AI-native digital studios. The competitive landscape is dominated by Oslo-based agencies, with Bergen having a smaller but emerging scene. Most competitors are more established, with dedicated sales funnels (pricing pages, booking flows, client logos). Falck Studios differentiates through its combination of AI, software, and creative projects (games, digital products), but the current site lacks social proof, case studies, and clear CTAs compared to competitors.

### Key Findings

1. **Market Position:** Falck Studios is a young entrant in a market with 5-10 direct competitors in Norway. Bergen hosts ~5% of Norway's AI companies, making it a small but growing hub.
2. **Design Gap:** Competitors universally feature client logos/testimonials, pricing pages, and strong CTAs. Falck Studios' site is visually clean but lacks these trust signals.
3. **Service Overlap:** AI solutions + custom software is the most common competitor offering. Falck Studios' creative/game development angle is a unique differentiator.
4. **Language:** Most competitors offer bilingual (NO/EN) sites. Falck Studios is Norwegian-only, which may limit international reach.
5. **Content:** Competitors feature case studies, blog posts, and detailed service pages. Falck Studios uses a single-page layout with minimal detail.

## Recommendations & New Tasks

Based on this research, the following improvements are recommended, ordered by impact:

### High Priority
1. **Add client testimonials / social proof section** — Every competitor has this. Even placeholder testimonials or partner logos would help.
2. **Add case studies or portfolio details** — The projects section currently only shows "coming soon." Adding real project details or mockups would significantly boost credibility.
3. **Add English language support** — Most competitors offer EN/NO. An English version would expand reach to international clients.
4. **Add a clear primary CTA** — Competitors use "Book a free meeting," "View Pricing," or "Contact Us" prominently. The current "Se hva vi gjør ↓" is passive.

### Medium Priority
5. **Create individual service pages** — Expand beyond the single-page layout with dedicated pages for AI, Software, and Creative services with more detail.
6. **Add a blog or knowledge base** — Content marketing is a key differentiator for Aone, TRIARE, and Lasting Dynamics.
7. **Implement a booking/contact form** — Replace or supplement the email-only contact with an inline form or scheduling tool.
8. **Add cookie consent banner** — Competitors in Norway all have GDPR-compliant cookie banners.

### Lower Priority
9. **Add a pricing page or packages overview** — Only Aone does this, but it's a strong conversion tool.
10. **Add a chat widget** — Aone and TRIARE use these for immediate engagement.
11. **Improve the projects section** — When projects launch, add screenshots, tech stack details, and links.

---

## Conclusion

Falck Studios has a clean, well-designed foundation with strong technical SEO. The main gaps compared to competitors are in **content depth** (case studies, blog), **trust signals** (testimonials, client logos), and **conversion optimization** (CTAs, booking flows, pricing). The creative projects angle (games, digital products) is a unique differentiator that no direct competitor currently offers — this should be leaned into more prominently once projects launch.

The Bergen market is less saturated than Oslo, giving Falck Studios an opportunity to establish itself as the go-to AI + creative studio in the region.
